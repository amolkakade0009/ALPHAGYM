Weight Loss Diet Plan

Vegetarian

Breakfast

Start your day with a high-fiber breakfast such as oatmeal or whole-grain toast with avocado.

Morning Snack

Have a small portion of nuts or a piece of fruit to keep your energy levels up.

Lunch

Opt for a light lunch with a salad or vegetable soup, and add a lean protein source like tofu or beans.

Afternoon Snack

Enjoy a Greek yogurt or a small portion of cottage cheese with some berries.

Dinner

Keep your dinner light with grilled vegetables and a lean protein like grilled chicken breast or fish.

Non-Vegetarian

Breakfast

Start your day with a high-protein breakfast such as scrambled eggs with vegetables or a protein smoothie.

Morning Snack

Have a small portion of lean turkey or chicken breast slices.

Lunch

Opt for a grilled chicken salad or a tuna salad with lots of greens and a light dressing.

Afternoon Snack

Enjoy a protein-rich snack such as a handful of almonds or a protein bar.

Dinner

Keep your dinner light with grilled fish or seafood paired with steamed vegetables.
